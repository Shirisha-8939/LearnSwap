Terms & Conditions

By creating an account you agree to the following rules.

1. Credits

Credits have no real monetary value and cannot be exchanged for cash. Credits transfer only when both teacher and learner confirm a session is complete.

2. Teacher Rules

  • Only list skills you genuinely possess.
  • Set an honest course plan (sessions and duration) when listing.
  • Attend sessions on time or notify the learner in advance.
  • Confirm completion only when a session truly happened.
  • Never request payment outside the credit system.

3. Learner Rules

  • Ensure you have enough credits before requesting a session.
  • Attend sessions or cancel at least 24 hours ahead.
  • Confirm completion only when the session genuinely took place.

4. Prohibited Conduct

  • No fake accounts, harassment, or abusive language.
  • No manipulation of the credit system.
  • No commercial transactions outside the credit system.
  • No sharing of another user's private messages without consent.

5. Disputes

Use the Dispute button on any session to report a problem. Repeated false disputes may result in account suspension.

6. Account Termination

LearnSwap may suspend or terminate accounts that violate these rules. Credits in a terminated account are forfeited.

Safety Guidelines

Your safety is our priority. Please follow these guidelines.

Protect Your Identity

  • Never share your home address, phone number, or bank details with other users.
  • Your email is private and never shown to other users.
  • You are not required to use your real full name as your display name.

Credit Safety

  • Credits never leave your account automatically — both parties must confirm.
  • If someone pressures you to confirm a session that did not happen, use the Dispute button.
  • LearnSwap will never ask for real money or bank information.

Report Abuse

  • Report users who ask for money outside the credit system.
  • Report hateful, harassing, or offensive messages immediately.
  • You can cancel any session at any time if you feel uncomfortable.
LearnSwap is built on trust and respect. Thank you for keeping it safe.
